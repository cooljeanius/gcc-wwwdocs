The GCJ Mailing Lists


Mail Archives

We have some gcj specific mailing lists set up. Click on the links below to view the archives.
java-announce
Low volume, moderated, announcements-only mailing list.
java-discuss
Moderate volume discussion/question mailing list.
java-testresults
Moderate volume test results mailing list.
java-patches
Moderate volume patch submission/discussion mailing list.
java-prs
Notification of new Problem Reports (PRs).
java-cvs
Notification of CVS commits. This list is not archived.

The archives are also available for downloading from ftp://sources.redhat.com/pub/java/mail-archives/.

Since gcj is also part of GCC, the GCC mailing lists are also relevant.
