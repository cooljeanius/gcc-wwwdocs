egcs project mailing lists

We are in the process of moving all mailing list functions from the Cygnus mailserver to the egcs machine.

This conversion has three key benefits for the egcs project.

  1. The delay between the time a message is posted to a list and the time it arrives at its final destination should be significantly reduced.
  2. We will be able to put in anti-spam filters on our lists without effecting Cygnus's mailserver.
  3. More of the list functions will be automated.

Please read the subscribe/unsubscribe instructions carefully. They may have changed since you last visited this page. They may also be different for certain lists as we move the lists to the egcs machine.

  • egcs-announce is a read-only low volume list where we post announcements about releases or other important events.
  • egcs is a high volume list for general discussions about egcs. Anything not covered by other mailing lists is suitable for discussion on egcs.
  • egcs-bugs is a relatively high volume list for bug reports and general discussion about bugs in egcs.
  • egcs-patches is a relatively high volume list for patch submissions and discussion of particular patches. All patch submissions and all discussion for a particular patch should be sent to egcs-patches.
  • egcs-cvs is a read-only, relatively high volume list which tracks checkins to the egcs CVS repository.
  • egcs-testresults is a moderate volume list where test results for the egcs compilers are posted. Note, there are no list archives for this list (yet).

All the lists have a maximum message size. If your message exceeds more than 40kbytes, you should compress any attachments before sending it to the list.

When posting messages, please select an appropriate list for the message and try to avoid cross posting a message to several lists.

Web archives for all mailing lists are on-line; egcs, egcs-announce, and egcs-bugs, egcs-cvs, egcs-patches.

Unfortunately the web archives are not searchable. We hope to replace Hypermail with another archiving engine with search capabilities in the future.

egcs, egcs-cvs, egcs-testresults and egcs-announce

These lists are run from the new list server on egcs.cygnus.com and thus work a little differently than the other lists.

To post a message, just send mail to listname@egcs.cygnus.com. Please refrain from sending messages in HTML, RTF or similiar formats.

To subscribe to a list, send mail to listname-subscribe@egcs.cygnus.com No message body is required.

To unsubscribe, send a mail note to listname-unsubscribe@cygnus.com No message body is required.

egcs-bugs, egcs-patches

To post a message, just send mail to listname@cygnus.com. Please refrain from sending messages in HTML, RTF or similiar formats.

To subscribe to a list, send mail to listname-request@cygnus.com with a message body consisting of

subscribe listname your-email-address

To unsubscribe, send a mail note to the appropriate -request@cygnus.com address with a message body of

unsubscribe listname your-email-address

Currently messages posted to the lists get delivered to list members 24 to 48 hours after submission to the list. As a result it may take a 24-48 hours after you subscribe before messages begin to flow, similarly it may take 24-48 after you unsubscribe before the messages stop.
