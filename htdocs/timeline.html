The egcs project maintained two kinds of version numbers. One was of the form 2.9x.yy and indicated the relationship between the GCC sources and the EGCS sources.

The other version number identified EGCS releases (as compared to development snapshots). So far, there have been releases 1.0.x, and 1.1.x.

After the April 1999 merger between GCC and EGCS, only a single version number is maintained.
