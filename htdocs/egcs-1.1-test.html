egcs-1.1 test strategy

This is very preliminary. Expect it to change and be refined as the steering committee settles on a test strategy for egcs-1.1.

  • No testsuite regressions on major platforms (to be defined) relative to egcs-1.0.3a or gcc-2.8.1 (both would be preferable).
  • libg++ add on builds and passes on same platforms as we do testsuite regressions on.
  • Builds glibc and passes glibc testsuite on major supported platforms (to be defined). We'll be testing a development version of glibc, not any currently released version. We will place a tag on the glibc sources to provide a common reference point for testing.
  • Linux kernel builds and runs (2.0.34 and 2.1.??? TBD). Platforms to be determined.
  • Red Hat 5.1 builds without errors on x86, Alpha (and Sparc?, PPC?).
  • RTEMS builds on the following RTEMS targets:
    • hppa1.1
    • i386, i386-elf, i386-go32
    • i960
    • m68k
    • mips64orion
    • powerpc
    • sh, sh-elf
    • sparc
