GCC Releases

Apart from providing our sources by means of CVS we also make source code for our releases available for FTP download.

Please use our main mirror site or any of our other mirrors.

Important: because these are source releases, they will be of little use to you if you do not already have a C compiler on your machine. If you don't already have a compiler, you need pre-compiled binaries.

To find pre-compiled binaries for various platforms, please refer to our binaries page.

Timeline

The egcs project maintained two kinds of version numbers. One was of the form 2.9x.yy and indicated the relationship between the GCC sources and the EGCS sources; the other version number identified EGCS releases (as opposed to development snapshots).

After the April 1999 merger between GCC and EGCS, only a single version number is maintained.
